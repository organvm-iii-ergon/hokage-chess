## üö® RED FLAGS (When to Pause & Reassess)

```
RED FLAG #1: CTR below 3% for 2+ weeks
ACTION: Stop uploading. Test 5 new thumbnail designs.
        Revert to best old video design until fixed.

RED FLAG #2: Retention drops below 40% at 30 seconds
ACTION: Hook is broken. Rewrite first 30 seconds.
        Test on 1 video. Compare CTR.

RED FLAG #3: Zero subscriber growth for 4+ weeks
ACTION: Major visibility problem. Do 1 collaboration.
        Change upload frequency or niche.

RED FLAG #4: Creator burnout (dreading filming)
ACTION: Reduce frequency immediately (2x/week vs 6x/week).
        Rest > content always.

RED FLAG #5: Community toxicity or disengagement
ACTION: Review moderation. Disable comments if needed.
        Focus on quality over growth temporarily.
```
