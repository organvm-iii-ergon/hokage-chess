## üéØ DECISION MATRIX: Content Approval

**Use this to decide IF a video idea is worth producing**

| Criterion | Weight | Score (1-5) | Notes |
|-----------|--------|-------------|-------|
| **Genuine Stakes** (will lose be real?) | 25% | ___ | Underdog > Guaranteed Win |
| **Narrative Arc** (ki-sho-ten-ketsu shape?) | 20% | ___ | Has setup, twist, resolution? |
| **Uniqueness** (different from last 5 videos?) | 20% | ___ | Is this angle novel? |
| **Engagement Potential** (comments/shares?) | 20% | ___ | Will people react/discuss? |
| **Series Continuity** (connects to larger arc?) | 15% | ___ | Does it advance Road to X? |
| | **TOTAL** | ___ / 100 | **Threshold: 60+ = Approve** |

**Example Application**:
- Video: "I Lost to a 900 Rated Player (Here's Why)" 
  - Genuine Stakes: 5 (real loss to lower-rated opponent)
  - Narrative Arc: 4 (setback leading to analysis)
  - Uniqueness: 3 (loss videos are common)
  - Engagement: 5 (people LOVE underdog failures)
  - Series Continuity: 3 (tangent from Road to 1500)
  - **TOTAL: 80/100 ‚Üí APPROVE**

## üö® RED FLAGS: When to Pivot

**STOP & Reassess if:**
- [ ] CTR drops below 3% for 2+ weeks (thumbnails broken)
- [ ] Average retention <40% at 30-second mark for 2+ videos (hooks failing)
- [ ] No subscriber growth for 4+ weeks despite consistent uploads
- [ ] Comments become toxic or engagement drops >50% month-over-month
- [ ] You burn out (unsustainable production pace detected)

**Pivot Actions**:
1. Take 1-week break to audit (don't publish)
2. Re-test with 5 videos using new thumbnails/titles/hooks
3. Ask community: "What should I do next?" (Discord poll)
4. Consider format change (more Shorts? Live streams? Tutorial series?)
